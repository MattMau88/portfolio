About Me

Hi, I'm Matt. I am eager to support organisations and individuals on their digital journeys. Appreciating to leverage technical capabilities and opportunities, I enjoy collaborating with cross-functional teams including diverse members and stakeholders. Moreover, as a Full Stack Developer I also enjoy designing, building and managing digital products and services hands-on.

I have an international background, cross-cultural understanding and global mindset resulting from both educational and professional experiences in several countries across Europe, North America and Asia, and I am experienced as Consultant, Operations and Project Manager, as well as trainer for IT solutions. In this regard, I have domain expertise in Application Performance Management (APM), Continuous Quality Assurance (CQA), IT Operations and End-to-end Monitoring to optimise Digital Performance and End-User Experience of applications.

In 2020, I decided to take some time to grow my technical expertise in Full Stack Coding and Data Science.

I've completed Le Wagon coding bootcamp in China (Shenzhen, Batch #410 - June 2020) to add technical skills in frontend and backend development to my profile (HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, Ruby on Rails, SQL, etc.). During the bootcamp I have invented, designed, coded, deployed and publicly pitched a team projects, as well as personal web apps and a WeChat Mini Program incl. Rails API. In addition to the bootcamp projects, I have also taken further Software Engineering courses online and built a recommendation system in Java.
